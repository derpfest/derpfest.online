Volunteer applications are now open!

An almost impossible task of successfully running a bilingual convention requires a large dedicated team. If you think that you have what it takes to be a part of such a unique occasion, please do join our ranks of volunteers.

This will let you take advantage of some exclusive volunteer team perks: free entrance, exclusive Derp Fest volunteer T-shirt, free lunch, a certificate of participation, a conbook mention, and an experience of a lifetime. So, whether you are an artist, a writer, a tech person, proficient in Russian and English, or even none of the above, but still willing to contribute, fill in the application form.
