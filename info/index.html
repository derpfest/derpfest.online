Derp Fest is an upcoming My Little Pony convention taking place on November 3rd-5th at Best Western Plus Vega Hotel and Convention Center in Moscow, the capital of Russia.

If you are interested in purchasing a ticket, please take a look at the ticket section for all the relevant information. We encourage you to purchase your ticket as soon as possible since ticket prices increase over time and ticket quantity is limited, especially if you are aiming for a sponsor-tier ticket.

We are currently working hard to get as many guests to attend, both guests of honor and community stars. You can take a look at who we have formally invited at the guests section.

If this is your first time traveling to Moscow or Russia, do not fret as we have prepared some handy guides regarding most aspects from getting a Russian visa to finding your way around Moscow, and even a shopping guide.

Let me also remind you that we are going to host a LARP on the 3rd November, which will show you how locals see the city as well as give you an opportunity to visit some lesser-known, but by far not less interesting sites.

If you are looking for a hotel in Moscow, you can either book a room at the venue by leaving a note on your ticket order or check the accommodation information page on recommended hotels and hostels.

Stay tuned for more information on our website and social media, as we will be publishing it as we get closer to the convention. If you still have any questions, feel free to contact any of our International Relations Department representatives:

We hope to see you at the convention in November!
