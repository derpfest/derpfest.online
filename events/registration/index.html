Derp Fest is run for the fans and by the fans. We want to make sure there is as large a variety of events there as possible, and for that we need your help. If you have an idea for a panel, a presentation, a workshop, or some other event, continue reading to find out how you can reach out to us and make your idea a reality!

Panel applications

If you wish to host a lecture, a discussion, or a showcase, your best option is to request a panel slot in the schedule. This format is more suitable if you believe you are going to attract a lot of people as the conference room can fit quite a lot. Please keep in mind that it may not be the most suitable option despite being the most obvious; if you host a panel, you are limited in time and the format of your event is strictly defined. If you still believe hosting a panel is the right choice for you, here is the application form.

Workshop hosting applications

If you want to show your skills and to teach people something new, a workshop may be a better option. You will be able to use a learning-by-doing method and give some unique tips and direct advice where necessary. Being able to ask a tutor is very important when learning something new and you may be able to inspire people to take up a new hobby or to help them improve their skills. If you need any specific equipment or materials, we may also be able to provide them. If you feel this resonates with your idea, then go on and sign up.

Activity zones

If none of the previously mentioned options suits your fancy, you can apply for an Activity Zone. This is the least restricted event format you can run at Derp Fest. You receive a designated area in the event halls where you can do pretty much anything.
