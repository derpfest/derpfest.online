Elias Frost

Multi-instrumental composer from the realms of the Northern internet. He is famous for the rock/metal covers of songs from My Little Pony as well as orchestra music inspired by Equestrian history. Elias mainly writes songs in English, although not exclusively. He also works in Finnish and Swedish languages and once spent a whole month doing covers of songs in German (Deutsch-November).

He doesn’t have many original works so far, but the most notable of them would be a collaboration with Jyc Row for the Ponies at Dawn project and a 25 minute folk/orchestra EP “Stories Vol. I”. You can find all of the songs Elias has made on his youtube channel.

HMage

HMage is a musician and a DJ. He has been following MLP since season one, but hadn’t been paying much attention to it until he heard PinkiePieSwear by FlutterWonder when it was released. This song impressed him so much, that he decided to create something of that kind. An experienced musician, he decided to join the brony fandom when he realised how many talented people already were among the herd.

He has since appeared on stage at RuBronyCon, GalaCon, Crystal Fair and BUCK.

Sweetie Bot project

Sweetie bot Project is a small team of enthusiastic brony engineers from Russia interested in ponies and robots and working together on designing and making a Sweetie Bot. Their mission, as the team describes it themselves, is to "take advantage of high tech magic to bring ponies into life, in robot form".

On Derp Fest they are planning to present a whole new prototype of ProtoPony3, the most advanced and complicated of their prototypes so far.
